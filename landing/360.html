Frequently Asked Questions

What are Active Profiles?
Active Profiles are the core entities you manage within your system, such as Customers, Products, Students, or Patients. Our pricing scales dynamically based on these active entities.
Can I change plans later?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your subscription at any time. Changes are applied instantly and billing is prorated to your next cycle.
Is there a free trial period?
Yes, all new accounts start with a 14-day free trial of our Growth Plan features. No credit card is required to get started. After the trial, you can choose to subscribe or cancel your service anytime.
What exactly can the Nexus AI Agent do for my business?
Think of it as your 24/7 digital employee. It automatically organizes your leads, predicts sales trends, and handles repetitive tasks like appointment bookings or data core management without breaks. On higher plans, we train the AI specifically on your business history, making it an expert that truly understands your unique operations.
