Refund Policy

Last Updated: February 26, 2026

1. Subscriptions

DigyNex follows a standard SaaS billing model. Once a subscription period (monthly or annually) has started, we generally do not offer refunds, but you can cancel any time to prevent future billing.

2. Errors in Billing

If you believe you have been billed in error, please contact our support team within 48 hours of the transaction. We will investigate and issue a full refund if a technical or administrative error occurred on our end.

3. Service Availability

Refunds or credits will be considered if our systems (TMS/CMS) experience unplanned downtime exceeding 5% in any given month.

4. How to Request

To request a refund, please email billing@digynex.se with your Organization ID and transaction details.
